Last updated: January 2025

At AutoMate AI, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines what data we collect and how we use it.

Analytics — We use basic analytics to understand which pages are most visited. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Cookies — We use minimal cookies for site functionality. No tracking cookies from third-party advertisers are used.

Some links on this site are affiliate links. When you click through and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. These links use standard affiliate tracking cookies.

Data retention

Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is anonymized and retained for 24 months.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in every email.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us through our contact page.
