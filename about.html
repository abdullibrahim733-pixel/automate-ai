About AutoMate AI

We test AI automation tools so you don't have to.

What we do

AutoMate AI is a independent publication dedicated to hands-on reviews of AI tools and automation technologies. We test every tool we write about — sometimes for weeks — before making a recommendation.

Our readers are developers, founders, and knowledge workers who want to work smarter. They come to us for honest, practical advice on which tools actually deliver on their promises.

Our approach

Every review follows a rigorous process:

  • Test in real workflows — We use each tool for actual tasks, not just demos
  • Compare against alternatives — No tool exists in isolation
  • Update regularly — The AI landscape changes weekly, and so do our reviews
  • Disclose everything — Affiliate links are marked. Sponsored content is clearly labeled. We don't take money for positive reviews.
